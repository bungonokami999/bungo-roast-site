The Cast Iron Difference

At Bungo Roast, we don't use industrial machines. We roast our beans right here at home using a seasoned cast iron frying pan. This hands-on, labor-intensive method allows us to control the roast profile intimately, unlocking depth and richness you simply can't find in mass-produced coffee.

Why We Love Shade Grown

We definitely prefer shade-grown coffee. By growing coffee plants under a canopy of trees, the cherries ripen more slowly. This adds an incredible depth and richness to the taste that our customers notice immediately. It also supports biodiversity and bird habitats!
